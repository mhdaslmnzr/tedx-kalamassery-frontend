Speaker Details

K.K Shailaja

Popularly known as the “coronavirus slayer” and the “rockstar health minister” , K.K Shailaja took the country by storm with her sensible judgement and effective protocols during the pandemic. The Former Minister for Health and Family Welfare of Kerala has received worldwide recognition for her efforts from organisations such as the UN, BBC, Vogue and Financial Times.

Dr. V S Priya

State's first Transgender Doctor, Priya has proved yet again the power of education over all other social restrains. Avowed male at birth, her early ages were a strife against herself and society did not make it any easy for her to win. Making education her utmost priority, Dr. Priya now lives in a society with equals, and has created a path for all the future kids that would not have to fight this battle again. Within history lies historic stories, and hers is definitely one of them.

Gayathri Rajeev

Singer, Reality Show Contestant, Gayathri Rajeev, has already become a household name. B.Tech student, and Karate Black Belt holder has stupefied all the state with her euphonic voice and is prized by her fans and supporters all across the state. With her realistic attitude, and unidealistic mannerisms, her story is all set to be known and loved as much as she is.

Ajayya Kumar

Ajayya Kumar, based in UAE, is the COO at Emircom. Ajayya is a qualified Cost Accountant from the prestigious Institute of Cost & Works Accountants of India (ICWAI) He is well known for his tactical skills and strategies, which makes him a trusted source for his bankers, vendors and fellow team members. He is a whiz in the field of finance and knows how to get the job done!

Anooja Bashir

Apostle for girl education, Director of FlexiCloud, Ourea, Woman Entrepreneur of the year and a 40 under 40 Awardee, Anooja Bashir has broken all the stereotypical circumvention to become most influential business woman. The entrepreneur guides people in Business Management field to create a customer-driven market, and with the same idea was her company able to withstand the Global Pandemic in style. Her achievements are multifarious and her objective only one, this CEO of the Year shares her story with the same intention to grow together.

Aditya Narayan

One of the youngest entrepreneur, CEO of Vieroots, Aditya Narayan is inspiration to this generations ideal call. He is continuously striving towards the 'call' and concurrently carrying forward his father, Dr. Sajeev Nair's legacy. Flamboyantly evincing this generation, enjoying with Bollywood and cricket biggies on one hand while on the other hand to transforming the lives of common people. He roots to find the best of everyone, and has created a first-ever personally modified lifestyle choices available within the reach of a touch. Some minor changes make a huge impact on selfhood and others around and these changes are that which makes this speaker unique.
